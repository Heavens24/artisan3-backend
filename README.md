# 🛠 Artisan 3.0

### AI-Powered Repair & Productivity Assistant

---

## 🚀 Vision

**Artisan 3.0** is building the future of **on-demand problem-solving** — empowering individuals and small businesses to diagnose, repair, and manage tasks using AI.

Our mission is simple:

> **Turn every smartphone into a smart technician.**

---

## 💡 Problem

Millions of people across emerging and developed markets face:

* ❌ High costs for basic repairs
* ❌ Limited access to skilled technicians
* ❌ Time wasted searching for solutions
* ❌ Lack of structured task management tools

---

## ✅ Solution

**Artisan 3.0** delivers an **AI-driven assistant** that:

* Diagnoses problems instantly
* Guides users through step-by-step fixes
* Manages tasks and workflows
* Provides a centralized knowledge system

All in one lightweight, accessible platform.

---
